Hi! I am Débora, an artist based in the San Francisco bay area, California. I love everything related to the visual arts, from photography, drawing, painting to animation, movies, and video game art. While studying to get a degree in 3D animation and Video Game art at Cañada and San Mateo Community Colleges, I had the opportunity to develop traditional art skills and grow an interest in wildlife and astro photography. Have a look at the work I have done, and get in touch to learn more. Thank you for your visit.
